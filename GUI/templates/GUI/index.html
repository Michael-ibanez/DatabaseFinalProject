Baylor University Gene Expression Data Tool

This is a tool to keep track of a study of gene expression at the messenger RNA (mRNA) level in different yeast strains. The mRNA is the intermediate between the DNA and protein. mRNAs are translated into protein. We measured RNA levels in different environmental conditions in order to determine how the levels change due to the environmental change.
